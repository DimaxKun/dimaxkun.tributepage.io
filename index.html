My Personal Tribute Page

Here's a time line of my life:

  • 2003 - I was born in Antipolo Rizal, Manila on October 27.
  • 2006 - I was 3 years old when we moved to Baguio City to pursue my siblings' education and more job opportunities for my parents.
  • 2009-2010 - I was 6 years old and started my education in kindergarten here in Baguio City. When I finished my kindergarten, I graduated in 2010.
  • 2015-2016 - I was then 12 years old, and started my 6th grade in elementary school. In 2016, I finally graduated from elementary school.
  • 2016-2017 - When I was 13 years old, I finally entered my high school era. This is where I finally noticed the difference in maturity of elementary & high school students.
  • 2019-2020 - I am now in my 4th year in high school (Grade 10). I am currently 16 years old, it is when the COVID pandemic started but also my graduation.
  • 2020-2021 - I'm finally through my high school era and I have now entered my Senior High School years through the devastating pandemic.
  • 2021-2022 - When I entered my senior high school I was really nervous especially its my first time studying in the University of the Cordilleras. But in August 2022, I managed to pull through and graduated from my course of STEM.
  • 2023 onwards - I am currently taking in my college year under the program of BSIT.
